About

From Los Angeles, CA, Doomsayer is a multi-genre bass producer carving a dark and uncompromising path through the underground. Built on a foundation of punishing frequencies and ritualistic energy, Doomsayer has taken his sound from the clubs of Nashville and Orlando to the floors of Las Vegas, Los Angeles, New York City and beyond.

Founder of Planet Doom, the biggest recurring EDM night in Knoxville, TN, Doomsayer has built not just a sound but a scene, summoning a congregation of the underground one ritual at a time.

He does not perform. He summons.
