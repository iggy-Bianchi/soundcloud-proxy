About

Hailing from Knoxville, TN, Doomsayer is a multi-genre bass producer carving a dark and uncompromising path through the underground. Built on a foundation of punishing frequencies and ritualistic energy, Doomsayer has taken his sound from the clubs of Nashville and Orlando to the floors of Las Vegas, Los Angeles, New York City and beyond.

He does not perform. He summons.

Every set is a ritual. Every drop is a summoning. The music does not ask to be heard. It arrives.
